Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

A chicken noodle soup recipe that's a very easy, very good substitute for when you don't have time to make your soup totally from scratch.

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken
  • 64 oz of bone broth
  • 1 lb of egg noodles
  • Carrots and Celery

    Directions

  1. Bake chicken at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
  2. Chop celery and carrots
  3. Bring the bone broth to a light boil and combine all ingredients
