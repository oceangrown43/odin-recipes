Alcapulco Shrimp Cocktail Recipe

Description

Bright, tangy, and bursting with a fiesta of texture, Campechana will instantly transport you to a beach state of mind. This easy and delicious grilled Mexican shrimp cocktail will have you popping open an icy cerveza and kicking back to chill.

    Ingredients

  • 7 Lemons
  • 3 Oranges
  • Tomatoes, Onions and Cilantro
  • Ketchup, Salt and Pepper

    Directions

  1. Boil Shrimp for 3 minutes or until cooked
  2. Chop Tomatoes, Onions and Cilantro
  3. Squeeze lemons and oranges then combine all ingredients
